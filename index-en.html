Our mission

A Yemeni youth council that believes in the principles of democracy and republicanism, and embraces a civil institutional vision of improving the skills and qualifications of Yemeni youth and build Yemeni cadres to ebable post-war recovery

Our objectives

1. Adopting and adapting an ideology that aims at establishing a civil state, in line with the values of the Yemeni society, and expanding it to all society’s segments as means to create a promising Yemeni future
2. Preparing qualified elites and cadres capable of leading the future of Yemen in various fields, with aim of achieving prosperity and aspirations of the people
3. Creating a platform for communication among Yemeni youth to exchange ideas and experiences with aim of crystallizing visions related to the future of Yemen
4. Strengthening the role of youth in the Yemeni arena in various fields
5. Helping Yemenis to achieve progress in a way that enables current and future generations to reap the fruits of this renaissance
